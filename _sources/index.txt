---------------------------
AftFore Documentation
---------------------------

:mod:`AftFore` is a python module for generating aftershock forecasts based on the method proposed in *Omi et al.*, (2013). Especially :mod:`AftFore` aims to improve the forecasts at the early stage (e.g. from the first few hours to the few days after the main shock).
